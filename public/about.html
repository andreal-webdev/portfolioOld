About me

My name is Andrea Larsen. I’m from Argentina, but I’ve been living in Canada for about 5 years.
Now, after working in the food industry for about 7 years, I decided to change my career. Even though I love cooking and decorating cakes, I realized that is not the best for my future. This industry is very demanding, and you need to work long hours, including holidays, and most of the times you are not well paid. So, after researching what other options I have. I decided to enrolled in the Multimedia Design and Development Program at Humber College. After the first semester, I realized that I enjoy coding. Once, I graduate I would like to works as a front end developer. I still have so much to learn, but I look forward to.

In my free time I like going to dance classes. My favourite style is jazz funk and ballet. During summer I like growing my own vegetables in my backyard. I think is very rewarding eating fresh veggies that are free fron pesticides. I also enjoy going for walks to with my dog. I have a 1 year old Berneddodle, a mis between bernese mountain dog and poodle. He loves being outdoors so our favourite season is summer.
